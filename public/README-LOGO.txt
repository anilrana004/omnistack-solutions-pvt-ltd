LOGO SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
=======================

Please place your logo file at:
  public/omnistack-logo.png

The logo will be used in:
  - Navbar (left side, 40x40px display)
  - Footer (left side, 40x40px display)

Recommended logo specifications:
  - Format: PNG with transparent background
  - Size: At least 200x200px (will be scaled down)
  - Aspect ratio: Square (1:1) works best

If you don't have a logo yet:
  - Create a simple "OS" lettermark or
  - Use a placeholder image
  - The site will work without it (you'll see a broken image icon until added)
